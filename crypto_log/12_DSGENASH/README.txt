DSGE Nash collects into a set of routines programs that the authors have used for 
their research in central banks and international institutions. 
The toolbox comes as it is. 
We assume no responsibility whatsoever for its use by third parties and make no
guarantees, implicit or explicit, about its quality, reliability, or any other characteristic. 
We would appreciate acknowledgment by citation of this working paper whenever the software
is used. 

The companion paper to this toolbox can be downloaded from here: 

https://www.ecb.europa.eu/pub/pdf/scpwps/ecb.wp2678~d03bdde9c8.en.pdf

An installation guide is included in the paper.
